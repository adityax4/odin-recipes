Samosa Recipe

Description

Delight your taste buds with the crispy and savory goodness of Samosas. These triangular pastries are filled with a flavorful mixture of spiced potatoes and peas, making them a popular snack across the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, ghee or oil, carom seeds, and salt. Knead into a firm dough using water. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and saut√© until they splutter.
  3. Add boiled and mashed potatoes, boiled peas, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Allow it to cool.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin oval shape.
  5. Cut each oval in half to form two semi-circles. Create a cone shape by folding each semi-circle and sealing the edges with water.
  6. Fill each cone with the prepared potato and pea mixture, then seal the open edge to form a triangular shape.
  7. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  9. Serve the crispy and delicious Samosas with mint chutney or tamarind sauce. Enjoy!
